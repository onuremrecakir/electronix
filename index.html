LM555 Timer Circuit

This circuit generates an oscillating signal using the versatile 555 Timer IC. It is commonly used in timing applications such as LED blinkers, pulse generators, and tone generators. You can adjust the frequency by modifying resistor and capacitor values.

Voltage Regulator

This circuit keeps the output voltage constant regardless of fluctuations in input voltage or output current. Often built around components like the 7805 regulator IC, it ensures that your components receive safe and consistent power.
